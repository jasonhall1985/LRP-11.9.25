How It Works

1. Camera Access

The app requests access to your phone's front-facing camera to capture your lip movements.

2. Lip Detection

Using MediaPipe Face Mesh, the app detects your face and extracts the lip region from each video frame.

3. Preprocessing

Lip regions are resized to 64x64 pixels, converted to grayscale, and normalized for the neural network.

4. Sequence Analysis

The CNN-LSTM model analyzes 30 frames of lip movement to predict which word was spoken.

Technical Details

Model Architecture

  • CNN layers for spatial feature extraction
  • LSTM layers for temporal sequence modeling
  • Input: 30 frames of 64x64 grayscale lip regions
  • Output: 5-class classification probabilities

Training Data

  • 20 videos per word (100 total training videos)
  • Separate validation and test sets
  • Cross-person generalization testing
  • Data augmentation for robustness

Technology Stack

  • MediaPipe for face/lip detection
  • TensorFlow/Keras for deep learning
  • Flask for web backend
  • HTML5 Canvas for video processing
